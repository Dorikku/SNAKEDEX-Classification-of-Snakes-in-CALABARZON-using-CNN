We are students of the Technological University of the Philippines.

Our project was inspired by the creators of the "Pokédex," who are fellow students from our school. Our system is intended for people navigating urban areas where different snake species can be encountered. We welcome any suggestions you may have, as we're working to make our system available in mobile offline mode for added convenience. Your input is important to us as we continue to improve our system.
